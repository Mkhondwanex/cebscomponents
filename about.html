About CEBS Components

Company Overview

Engineering Excellence Since 2011

CEBS Components (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned engineering services company specializing in steel fabrication, CNC turned components, and comprehensive engineering solutions for mining, power stations, and the automotive industry.

Founded by Blessing Boysie Cebekhulu, our company brings over 23 years of industry expertise to every project we undertake. From precision manufacturing of truck components to large-scale mining infrastructure, we deliver with excellence.

Our vision is to become Africa's largest engineering services provider, expanding our reach to international clients while maintaining our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Founder & CEO

Blessing Boysie Cebekhulu was born and raised in KwaZulu-Natal, Eshowe in 1979. After matriculating in 1997, he pursued his passion for engineering by studying mechanical fitting at Richtek.

His career began as an apprentice at Sasol in Secunda, where his exceptional skills and dedication led to him obtaining a Red Seal qualification in 2011. He further expanded his expertise as a rigger artisan in Olifantsfontein.

A significant milestone in Blessing's career was his participation in the installation of the world's largest homogenizing furnace at Bayside Aluminum in 2003. Working alongside IUT Sweden, led by Bjonn Hedendal, this project showcased his ability to execute complex engineering tasks at the highest level.

With over 23 years in the engineering business, Blessing has developed CEBS Components from a vision into a respected engineering services provider. His leadership, technical knowledge, and commitment to excellence continue to drive the company toward becoming Africa's premier engineering services company.
